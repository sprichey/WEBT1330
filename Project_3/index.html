Spring Art Festival

In partnership with the Metropolitan Cultural Trust, the Community Arts Council has reimagined the Spring Art Festival with a new vision. This free event features four weekends of music, theater, dance, gallery exhibitions, an artist’s market, food, and more. Focusing on local and regional artists, the annual festival celebrates the rich cultural heritage and creative diversity that makes our city unique. More...

Kids' Workshops

 

The Community Arts Council offers a variety of opportunities for children up to 12 years old to exercise their imaginations, experience a variety of different media, and explore their natural creative sides. All children’s classes are held immediately after school hours, or on weekend mornings, at the Artists’ Lofts Open Studio. Classes are limited to six students per instructor, so register early. Adult Enrichment Classes

 

Whether you want to explore a new hobby, experiment with a new medium, or enhance existing skills, our eight-week adult classes provide in-depth, personal instruction, and guidance by masters in their media. Class sizes are limited to maximize the personal learning experience. Classes require no prior knowledge or experience; all necessary materials are included in the registration fee. More...
