Join us!

Opened in 2009 by award-winning chef Elizabeth Frank and local olympic medalist Sylvia Bergman, Frank & Bergman Fresh Bistro is one of the brightest dining spots in the Valley. Fresh farm-to-table ingredients, rotating seasonal menus, and daily and weekly specials bring you delicious, affordable cuisine that takes advantage of the area's diverse agricultural offerings.

Enjoy a warm desert evening on the outdoor, pet-friendly patio, or come in and enjoy the post-industrial architecture in our recently remodeled dining room. In addition to comfortable, intimate seating in the main dining room, we also offer a countertop bar and separate communal high bar for a more casual experience.
